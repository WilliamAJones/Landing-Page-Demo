My Journey

I graduated from Park City High School in Park City, Utah in 2004. After multiple major changes, and three school changes, I graduated from BSU in 2010 with a History Degree

My first career ended far quicker than it began. After deciding I did not want to become a school teacher I moved to Seattle, WA to pursue new passions. Having always been an avid gamer with a basic knowledge of computers I was able to easily transition into a retail sales role at both Staples and BestBuy in the Computer Department, later I used the skills I learned there to transition to a more technical oriented role with Aiphone, Japanese telecom company, based in Bellevue,WA

I moved back to Boise in 2015 to be closer to family. After working in tech support for companies in the Boise area who provide remote support for both public and private sector clients I decided it was time for the next step in my career. I boiled my options down to two choices, and chose the one that allowed for more creativity
